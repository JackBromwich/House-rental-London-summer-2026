# Areas Shortlist: 1-Bed Flat near Hoxton, £1,400/month

Budget: £1,400/month | Property type: Whole 1-bed flat (no house shares) | Target: May–December 2026

---

## Recommended Areas (Within Budget)

### 1. Walthamstow (E17) — Best Overall Pick
- **Price range:** £1,100–£1,650/month
- **Travel to Hoxton:** ~10–15 min via Overground (Walthamstow Central → Hoxton)
- **Notes:** Most supply at this budget, frequent Overground service, lively high street and village area

### 2. Leyton (E10) — Budget Sweet Spot
- **Price range:** £1,350–£1,400/month
- **Travel to Hoxton:** ~12–18 min via Overground or Central Line + bus
- **Notes:** Right at the budget ceiling, quieter than Walthamstow, improving area

### 3. Stoke Newington (N16) — Best Neighbourhood Feel
- **Price range:** £1,200–£2,200/month (average ~£1,800 — hunt the lower end)
- **Travel to Hoxton:** ~12–20 min via buses 76, 149, or 243
- **Notes:** No direct tube, but good bus frequency; requires patience to find sub-£1,400 listings

### 4. Elephant & Castle (SE1) — South London Fallback
- **Price range:** £1,100–£1,600/month
- **Travel to Hoxton:** ~25–35 min (Northern/Bakerloo + connections)
- **Notes:** Under budget, major regeneration area, but commute is at the edge of 30 min

## Search Tips

- Use **Rightmove** and **Zoopla**, filter: property type = Flat, max £1,400, 1 bed
- Always verify in the listing description it's a **whole flat**, not a room in a shared house
- Set up **email alerts** for E17, E10, N16 — good flats go fast
- Start viewing **late March/early April** for a May move-in
- Key transport hub: **Hoxton Overground station** (East London Line) — prioritise areas with direct Overground links
